About us

Techno Philippines Innovation Corp. was established in 2016 in Cabanatuan City Nueva Ecija. We are provider of high-level Security Systems, Home Automation (Internet of Things) Systems, and Renewable Energy Systems. We are committed to you in the strategic design and seamless execution of innovative, state-of-the-art technology solutions that are delivered within budget and agreed time scales. Our core strength is based on Techno Philippines investing in its people and its products to protect your people and your assets with continued growth throughout the Philippines. Techno Philippines Innovation Corp. world-class technology is supported by highly experienced technology specialist's partners, service and maintenance professionals all furnished with years of experience in the integration of major systems.
